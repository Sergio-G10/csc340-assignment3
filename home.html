Hello to all the players using our app! We want to bring some good news about a tourament that we have been planning for a while now. The very first tournament hosted by Pickup Soccer will be taking place on August 23rd, 2024! We want to thank everyone for the support you have shown this app and look forward to seeing you on the pitch! More information can be found below!

  • The games will be 7v7 on a 3/4 sized field.

  • All participants must be 16+.

  • The games will be 7v7 on a 3/4 sized field.

  • Any participants less than 18 years old will need a form signed by their parent and the parent will need to be present for the duration of the participant's stay.

  • The tournament will be a simple knockout tournament, with teams randomly chosen with opponents.

  • Registration will be $10 per player.
